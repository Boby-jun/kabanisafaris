About Kabani Safaris

A Consultant
Rooted in Africa

After four years living in East Africa and based in Nairobi, Kenya, I built a deep network of local guides, operators, and farmers who know their terrain like the back of their hand. Kabani Safaris is not a booking platform — it is a consultancy that matches your curiosity with the right people on the ground.

"Every itinerary is built from scratch. The goal is always the same: a memory that outlasts the journey."

A Special Focus

Coffee at the
Heart of It All

African coffee is not just a product — it is a culture, an economy, and a community. Wherever an itinerary allows, I integrate coffee cooperative visits so travellers can witness firsthand the hands behind the harvest.

  • Tour washing stations and dry mills with the farmers who run them
  • Visit cooperatives representing thousands of smallholder producers across Kenya, Ethiopia, and Rwanda
  • Participate in cuppings, roastings, and processing demonstrations
  • Understand how speciality coffee economics flow back to origin communities
  • Leave with relationships — and exceptional beans — that tell a real story
